About festival

What has adorned him all the past years is the fact that he was wide open to all the cinemas of the world, to all relevant authors, but also to movies that will not be found on the cinema repertoire. The importance of FEST is reflected in the fact that the Belgrade audience - with the films of some of the greatest directors of today such as Theo Angelopulos, Bob Rafelson, Volker Schlendorf, David Kronenberg, Ken Russell, Vim Venders, Kislovsky, Hercog ... had the opportunity to meet for the first time on FEST.

The first FEST started to be quite spectacular, with "Odyssey in the Universe" by Stanley Kubrick, "Naked in the Saddle" by Denis Hopper, the "Meš" by Robert Altman, the films by Erik Romer, Louis Bunuel, Zivojin Pavlović ... Although many, from nostalgia but rightly, consider the FEST's first edition to be unmatched, the truth is that in the coming years we would watch films that will remain recorded in the history of world cinema in FEST in the coming years. In the history of the festival, numerous great actors and directors of the festival were also included: Marcelo Mastrojani, Harvey Kaytel, Liv Ulman, Bernardo Bertolucci, Sofia Loren, Robert De Niro, Antonioni, Johnny Depp, Milos Forman, Katrin Danev, Vim Venders , Abaz Kjarostami, Hana Šigula, previously mentioned authors Rafelson, Schlendorf, Russell ... the list is very impressive, honorable and long.
