Julia Roberts

Basic

Julia Roberts, 28 October 1967 in Smyrna, Georgia, is an American film actress and former model. She gained world fame in 1990, thanks to the main role in the drama "Pretty Woman", and later in roles in romantic comedies. The most famous of them are the wedding of my best friend, I believe in love and Rude Young. For the role in the drama Erin Brokovic, from 2000, she received the Golden Globe, the BAFTA Award and the Oscar for Best Actress.

Childhood and education

Julia was born in Smyrna, not far from Atlanta as Julia Fiona Roberts. Her father was Walter Grede Roberts, and mother Beti Lu. The two of them divorced in 1971, after which Betty marries Michael Moutes. Six years later, Walter died of cancer. Julia has an older brother Erika, who is also an actor. As a child, she wanted to become a veterinarian, but after completing the high school "Campbell" and attending "George's University", she went to New York to act. She went to acting classes, and worked as a model.

Career

As early as 1990, Roberts got world fame in the role of a romantic drama, "Pretty Woman", for which she won the Golden Globe for Best Actress and the second Oscar nomination. For this role, Julia won the competition composed by Deril Hannah and Meg Ryan, who was then a big star of love drama. The theme of the film is a romance that is born between a prostitute and a businessman, interpreted by Richard Gir. This film earned more than $ 460,000,000 worldwide, making it the fourth-ranked romantic comedy with the highest earnings, and is considered to be the most financially most successful film in Julie's career. The following year, she played the nurse Hilary in the drama "Die Young", then in the movie "In Bed with an Enemy" and Zvoncica in Spielberg's "Kuki", after which she stopped recording for a while. Magazine Pipl subsequently published the article "What happened to Julia Roberts?", Which caused a lot of controversy in public. In 1993, she received the role of Eni Reid in the film "The Besana in Seattle", but she refused in favor of Meg Ryan.

Private life

Julius Roberts's private life has been in the forefront of interest ever since her career began. She was in contact with Liam Nison, Kifera Saderland, Jason Patrick, Matthew Perry, Benjamin Brett and many others. She was even faithful to Saderland, but she broke up on her three days before the wedding and traveled to Ireland with his friend, Jason Patrick. In 1993, she married Lyle Lavet, and in 1995 they divorced. Today she is married to cameraman Daniel Modera. They met on the film "Mexican" and married on June 4, 2002. They have three children.
