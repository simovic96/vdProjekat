Leonardo DiCaprio

Basic

Leonardo Wilhelm Dikaprio (Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio, Los Angeles, November 11, 1974) is an American actor and producer. He was nominated five Oscars for Best Actress in the Avijatičar (2004) films, Bloody Diamond (2006), Vuk with Vol Strita (2013) and Povratnik (2016), for which he received this award. He was also nominated for the best actor in a supporting role in the film What Eats Gilbert Grape (1994), four times for the BAFTA Award, and eleven times for the Golden Globe Award.
