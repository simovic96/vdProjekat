About festival

This year's Belgrade International Film Festival will more than ever feature a bridge between domestic, dominantly commercial, cinema repertoire and the most important films created between two festival editions. Film directors from all parts of the world during ten festival days will again be the main topic of the audience, which has built its good taste right at this festival with almost half a century of tradition. From the titles that have already been recognized and awarded in the world to a performance whose appearance on Fest will be, it is quite certain, the only public projection in this part of our planet, they are all present in festival halls throughout Belgrade, a city that from 23rd to 4th 3. In 2018 it again becomes the capital of the film.
