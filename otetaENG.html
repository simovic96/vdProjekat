About movie

96 hours Taken is a French action thriller film from 2008 in English by director Pierre Morel. Scenarists are Lick Beson and Robert Mark Cayman. Producer of the film is Lik Beson. The music was composed by Nathaniel Mixali.

The cast includes Liam Nison, Maggie Grace, Famke Jansen, Liland Orser, John Grajs, David Varschowski, Kate Cassidy and Holly Valans.

The film earned more than $ 226 million. Numerous media have listed the film as a turning point in Nison's career that redefined and transformed it into an action movie star.
