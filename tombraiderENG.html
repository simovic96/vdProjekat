Tomb Raider

About movie

Tomb Raider is an American action adventure film from 2018, directed by Rara Utaug on the 2013 video game of the same name. Film producer is Graham King. The script is signed by Jeneva Robertson Dvort and Elastir Sidons. The music was composed by Janky XL.

The title role is interpreted by Allysia Vikander as Lara Croft, and in other roles are Dominic West, Volton Gogins, Daniel Vu, Kristin Scott Tomas, Derek Jakobi and Nik Frost. The World Premiere of the film was held on March 14, 2018 in the United Kingdom, while it was launched in the United States on March 16, 2018.

The film's budget was $ 94,000,000. Lara Kroft (Alice Wikander) is a passionate, independent girl and daughter of an eccentric adventurer who disappeared while she was still a teenager. Now, after seven years, Lara has no real goal or purpose, she refuses to take over the reins of her father's empire, just as she refuses to admit that he actually went away.

Opposing his father's last wishes, Lara decides to leave everything behind to solve the mystery of his mysterious disappearance, and begins his quest by exploring the last location where he was seen.

The series of games has experienced two film adaptations, Lara Croft: The Robber's Tomb in 2001 and the 2003 Cradle of Life. The releases of the third film appeared in 2007 and it was officially announced for 2009. Film rights were purchased by GK Film in 2011, and in 2015 they confirmed the selection of directors and production of the film by GK Films, Vorner Bros and MGM studies. An animated series based on the character Lara Kroft appeared in 2007 and is broadcast by the Game Tap online game service, as part of a series of revival of popular video games. It was broadcast between May and June 2007 under the name Tomb Raider: Revision. Although the production team had great creative freedom, Lara Kroft was not allowed to deviate from the original concept.
